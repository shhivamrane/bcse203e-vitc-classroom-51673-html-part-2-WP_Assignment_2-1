Ingredients

  • 200g of Pasta
  • 100g of Tomato Sauce
  • 50g of Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 clove of Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil Leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 350 kcal per serving
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Sodium: 450mg
